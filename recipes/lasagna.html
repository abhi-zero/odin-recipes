Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian pasta dish made with layers of pasta sheets, cheese, and a rich meat or vegetable sauce. It's a hearty and comforting meal.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • 500g minced meat or vegetables
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan

Steps

  1. Cook lasagna sheets according to package instructions.
  2. Prepare the sauce with minced meat, vegetables, and marinara.
  3. Layer pasta sheets, sauce, and cheese in a baking dish.
  4. Repeat layers and top with mozzarella and Parmesan.
  5. Bake at 180Â°C for 30-40 minutes.
  6. Let cool slightly before serving.
