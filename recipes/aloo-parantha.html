Aloo Paratha

Description

Aloo Paratha is a popular North Indian breakfast dish made with unleavened dough stuffed with a spicy mashed potato filling. It's usually served with yogurt and pickle.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups wheat flour
  • 3 boiled potatoes
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander
  • Butter or ghee for frying

Steps

  1. Prepare dough with wheat flour, water, and salt; set aside.
  2. Mix boiled potatoes with spices and coriander for the filling.
  3. Roll out dough, place filling inside, and seal.
  4. Roll out again gently and cook on a hot skillet with ghee.
  5. Serve hot with yogurt and pickle.
