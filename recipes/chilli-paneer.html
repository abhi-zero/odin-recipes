Chilli Paneer

Description

Chilli Paneer is a popular Indo-Chinese dish made with fried paneer cubes tossed in a spicy, tangy sauce with vegetables. It can be served as an appetizer or main course.

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp chili sauce
  • 1 tsp garlic paste
  • Cornflour for coating

Steps

  1. Coat paneer cubes in cornflour and shallow fry until golden.
  2. Saut√© garlic, onions, and bell peppers in oil.
  3. Add soy sauce, chili sauce, and paneer cubes.
  4. Mix well and cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with fried rice or noodles.
